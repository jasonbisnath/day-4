"VIRGIL ALBOH" THE STORY OF OFF-WHITE & THE FACE OF FASHION & IT'S CULTURE

There are two things that I think define powerful creativity: curiosity and obsession.

Our obsession with serving the athlete and building cutting-edge, pioneering performance products coupled with Virgil’s intense curiosity, allowed us to, together, recontextualize these iconic shoes for a new generation of Nike enthusiasts.

Many of our icons are classics, and our ability to iterate allows them to remain as distinctly relevant and as culturally in stride now, as they were when they were first introduced.

The future insists that we build products that are distinct and new, remaining constantly on the precipice of improvement.

But, being distinct is actually quite difficult in modern culture, so that’s where we leverage the power of innovation through sport.
